How do I update my profile information? ▼
To update your profile information, go to your account settings from the user menu in the top right corner. Click on "Profile Settings" and you can edit your personal information, skills, portfolio, and other details.
How do I change my password? ▼
Navigate to Account Settings > Security. Click on "Change Password" and enter your current password followed by your new password. Make sure your new password is strong and unique.
How do I post a new project? ▼
Click on "Post a Project" from your dashboard. Fill out the project details including title, description, budget, timeline, and required skills. Review your information and publish to start receiving proposals from freelancers.
How do I select the right freelancer? ▼
Review proposals carefully, check freelancer profiles, ratings, and previous work samples. Consider their communication skills, proposed timeline, and budget. You can also conduct interviews before making your final decision.
What payment methods are accepted? ▼
We accept major credit cards, PayPal, bank transfers, and various digital payment methods. You can add or update your payment methods in the billing section of your account settings.
How does the escrow system work? ▼
Our escrow system holds your payment securely until the work is completed satisfactorily. Funds are released to the freelancer only after you approve the delivered work, ensuring protection for both parties.
What should I do if there's a problem with my project? ▼
First, try to resolve the issue directly with the freelancer through our messaging system. If that doesn't work, you can open a dispute through the project page, and our mediation team will help resolve the matter fairly.
How long does dispute resolution take? ▼
Most disputes are resolved within 5-7 business days. Complex cases may take longer. Our mediation team reviews all evidence and communications to make fair decisions based on our terms of service.
